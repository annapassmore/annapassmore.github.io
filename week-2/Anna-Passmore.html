About Me

I was born and raised in Ottawa, Ontario, and lived here until the age of 14 when I moved accross the country to attend boarding school. I shortly retunred to Ottawa following my grade 10 year. I have been a competitive irish dancer since I was 8 competing around North America in places like Toronto, New York, and Boston.

I was also a concert pianist since I was 7 which lead to doing Royal Conservatory practical and theory exams achieving honours in all exams. I sadly had to quit both dance and piano in grade 12 due to school and work. But I have since found ways to stay in shape and keep me busy. Whenever I find the chance, I will still dance at my home or play piano for fun. As well as I now bury myself in work and found a new love for the gym.
